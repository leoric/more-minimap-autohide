## Tired of the UI getting cut off on smaller screens? 

This mod automatically hides the minimap when you hover over certain entities, giving your sidebar more breathing room. Now you can easily read your game UI and logistics network details without the minimap blocking the view!

### How it Works
* **Auto-Hide:** The minimap disappears whenever your mouse hovers over logistics chests, trains, cargo wagons, roboports, mining drills, assembling machines, labs, and more. As soon as you move your mouse away, the minimap returns.
* **Space Platforms:** The minimap is disabled by default on space platforms (this can be toggled in the settings).

---

### Compatibility Notes

> ⚠️ **Important Note for Fire Lights Users:**
> There is a known conflict with the **"Remove Space Platform Minimap"** option in Fire Lights. Please **disable** that setting in Fire Lights and use the built-in setting from this mod instead.

* **Fire Lights (Night Settings):** Fully supported. The minimap will respect night settings and won't unexpectedly pop back up.

### New Hotkeys

This mod also adds new hotkeys to permanently toggle the visibility of various UI elements. If you need to clear up even more screen space, you can use:

* **`CTRL` + `M` (Map):** Toggles the display of the minimap on the sidebar.
* **`CTRL` + `T` (Technology):** Toggles the research progress display at the top left (very useful if you have nothing left to research).
* **`CTRL` + `B` (Toolbar):** Toggles the bottom toolbar and the bottom-right armor/ammo bar.
* **`CTRL` + `N` (Notifications):** Toggles the alerts displayed to the right of the toolbar (e.g., missing construction items, damaged entities).

> ⚙️ **Customization:** All of these hotkeys can be rebound to your preference by going to **Options** -> **Controls** -> **Mods**.
